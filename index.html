1. SHAPE SELECTION

Single Hand: Show 1-5 fingers to select a shape.

1. Make a FIST with ONE hand.

Use Both hands to Select a Shape.

Move TOGETHER to Zoom In.

4. TIPS

Ensure your hand is clearly visible and well-lit.
